nivigispi: A Custom Time Unit

Introduction to nivigispi

Nivigispi is a custom time unit where 1 nivigispi = 30 seconds. This unit is designed to offer a simpler way to measure short intervals of time, making it easier to conceptualize seconds in batches. It can be especially useful for counting break times.
